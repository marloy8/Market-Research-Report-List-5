<p><p>Household solar panels market consists of two main types: Monocrystalline Solar Panels and Polycrystalline Solar Panels. Monocrystalline panels are made from single silicon crystals, known for their efficiency and sleek design. Polycrystalline panels are made by melting multiple silicon crystals together, offering a cost-effective option with slightly lower efficiency. Both types of panels are popular choices for residential solar installations, providing sustainable energy solutions for households looking to reduce their carbon footprint and save on electricity bills.</p></p>
