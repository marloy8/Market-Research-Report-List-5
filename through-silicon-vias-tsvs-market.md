<p><p>Through-Silicon Vias (TSVs) are vertical interconnects that pass through a silicon wafer, enabling connections between stacked semiconductor dies. The 2.5D TSV market involves stacking multiple dies on a silicon interposer, while the 3D TSV market involves stacking multiple dies directly on top of each other. Both technologies provide increased performance, reduced form factor, and higher functionality in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and data centers. They are key enablers for advanced packaging solutions in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
