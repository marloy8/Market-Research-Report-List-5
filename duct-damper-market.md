<p><p>Duct damper market consists of two main types: manual dampers and electric dampers. Manual dampers are operated manually to control the airflow in duct systems, while electric dampers are controlled electronically. Manual dampers are commonly used in residential settings due to their cost-effectiveness, simplicity, and ease of installation. On the other hand, electric dampers are preferred in commercial or larger buildings for their ability to be integrated into automated HVAC systems for more precise control and energy efficiency.</p></p>
