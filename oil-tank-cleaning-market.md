<p><p>Oil tank cleaning market can be divided into two main types: manual cleaning and automatic cleaning. Manual cleaning involves physically entering the tank and removing sludge and residue using tools and equipment. This method is labor-intensive and time-consuming, but it allows for thorough cleaning. On the other hand, automatic cleaning involves the use of specialized machines and equipment to clean the tank without the need for human intervention. This method is faster and more efficient, but may not always be suitable for all types of tanks.</p></p>
