<p><p>Oil Pressure Regulators (OPR) are used in hydraulic systems to regulate the pressure of the oil flowing through the system. Single-stage regulators are designed to maintain a constant pressure output, while multi-stage regulators offer more precise control by allowing for adjustments at different stages. Single-stage regulators are simpler and more cost-effective, while multi-stage regulators provide greater accuracy and flexibility. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the optimal performance and efficiency of hydraulic systems across various industries.</p></p>
