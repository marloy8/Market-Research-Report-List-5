<p><p>Term Life Insurance Market Types typically include Renewable Term Life Insurance and Return Premium Term Life Insurance.</p><p>Renewable Term Life Insurance allows policyholders to renew their coverage at the end of the term without undergoing a medical exam, but usually at a higher premium. This type of policy is ideal for those who may experience health changes over time.</p><p>On the other hand, Return Premium Term Life Insurance refunds a portion of the premiums paid by the policyholder if the policy expires without a death benefit being paid out. This type of policy offers a financial incentive for those who outlive the term of the policy.</p></p>
