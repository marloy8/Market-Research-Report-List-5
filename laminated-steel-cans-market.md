<p><p>Laminated steel cans are commonly used for packaging food and beverages. There are two main types of laminated steel cans: 2-piece cans and 3-piece cans. </p><p>2-piece cans are made from a single piece of steel that is formed into a can body and bottom. These cans are popular for their ease of production and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>3-piece cans, on the other hand, are made from three separate components - a can body, top, and bottom. These cans are commonly used for products that require a higher level of protection and durability.</p></p>
