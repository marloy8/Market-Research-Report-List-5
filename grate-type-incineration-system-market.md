<p><p>Grate type incineration systems are categorized into three main types: Reciprocating Grate Stoker, Step Grate Stoker, and Roller Grate Stoker. Reciprocating Grate Stokers have moving grates that oscillate back and forth to facilitate even burning. Step Grate Stokers have stationary grates with steps to allow for better control of air flow. Roller Grate Stokers feature rotating grates to efficiently burn waste material. Each type of system offers unique advantages and is suitable for different types of waste incineration processes.</p></p>
