<p><p>Truck mixers are classified into three main categories based on their load capacity: below 6 ton, 6-15 ton, and above 15 ton. Truck mixers in the 6-15 ton range are more versatile and are commonly used in medium-sized construction projects. Truck mixers above 15 ton are heavy-duty vehicles capable of carrying large volumes of concrete for major infrastructure projects.</p></p>
