<p><strong>The Automatic Textile Screen Print Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line</li><li>Carousel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic textile screen print machines can be categorized into two main types: in-line and carousel. In-line machines operate in a linear fashion, with each station carrying out a different step in the printing process. Carousel machines, on the other hand, feature a rotating circular platform with multiple stations, allowing for simultaneous printing on multiple garments. Both types offer efficiency and accuracy in textile printing, with carousel machines often favored for their ability to handle high volumes of work.</p></p>
