<p><p>The golf wedge market consists of four main types: pitching wedge, gap wedge, sand wedge, and lob wedge. Each type is designed for a specific purpose on the golf course. The pitching wedge is used for shorter shots with higher trajectory, the gap wedge fills the yardage gap between the pitching wedge and sand wedge, the sand wedge is designed to help players escape sand traps, and the lob wedge is used for high, soft shots around the green. Each wedge type caters to different aspects of the game for golfers of all skill levels.</p></p>
