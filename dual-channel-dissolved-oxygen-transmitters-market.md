<p><p>Dual-channel dissolved oxygen transmitters are available in different market types such as 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire configurations. The 2-wire transmitter requires only two wires for both power and communication, making it simpler to install and cost-effective. The 3-wire transmitter offers improved stability and accuracy compared to the 2-wire system, with separate wires for power and communication. The 4-wire transmitter provides the highest level of accuracy and precision, as it separates power and communication onto separate pairs of wires.</p></p>
