<p><ul><li>Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging(FO WLP)</li><li>Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging(FI WLP)</li><li>Flip Chip(FC)</li><li>2.5D/3D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor Advanced Packaging Market Types include Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FO WLP), Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FI WLP), Flip Chip (FC), and 2.5D/3D packaging. FO WLP involves redistributing connections from the chip to the package, increasing functionality and performance. FI WLP places the chip in the center of the package, enabling higher density and smaller form factor. Flip Chip technology directly connects the chip to the package, improving speed and power efficiency. 2.5D/3D packaging stacks multiple chips vertically, enhancing performance and reducing footprint.</p></p>
