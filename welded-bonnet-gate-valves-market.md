<p><ul><li>Threaded End</li><li>Seal Welded End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Threaded end gate valves have a female threaded connection on one or both ends, allowing for easy installation or removal using compatible male threaded pipe fittings. Seal welded end gate valves have the ends welded shut, providing a permanent seal and preventing leakage. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications where a reliable and secure shut-off valve is needed. Threaded end gate valves are preferred for applications where frequent maintenance or replacement is required, while seal welded end gate valves are ideal for situations where a permanent seal is necessary.</p></p>
