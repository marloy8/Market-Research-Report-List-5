<p><strong>The Haptics Feedback Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators</li><li>Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAS)</li><li>Drivers & Controllers</li><li>Software</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Haptic feedback technology market encompasses various types such as Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators, Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs), Drivers & Controllers, Software, and others. ERM actuators use an unbalanced weight on a motor shaft to produce vibrations, while LRAs use a magnetic voice coil attached to a spring mass system. Drivers & controllers regulate the intensity and frequency of vibrations, while software enables customization of haptic effects. Other technologies in this market may include piezoelectric actuators or electroactive polymers.</p></p>
