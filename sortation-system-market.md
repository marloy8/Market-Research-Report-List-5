<p><p>Sortation systems are commonly used in warehouses and distribution centers to streamline the process of sorting and diverting items to their respective destinations. There are two main types of sortation systems on the market: linear sortation systems and loop sortation systems. Linear sortation systems feature a straight conveyor line where items are sorted and diverted at various points along the line. Loop sortation systems use a continuous loop conveyor to sort and divert items as they circulate through the system. Both systems offer efficient and accurate sorting capabilities for various industries.</p></p>
