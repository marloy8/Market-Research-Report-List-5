<p><p>In the Hi-Flo Stopcock market, there are four main types available - 4-way, 3-way, 2-way, and 1-way stopcocks. A 4-way stopcock allows for connectivity to four different fluid lines, making it versatile for various medical procedures. A 3-way stopcock is commonly used for medication delivery or blood transfusions. A 2-way stopcock is simple and efficient for basic fluid control. A 1-way stopcock is ideal for restricting flow in a single direction. Each type caters to specific needs in medical settings.</p></p>
