<p><ul><li>BEOL</li><li>FEOL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor wafer market types include Backend of Line (BEOL) and Frontend of Line (FEOL). BEOL involves the processes and materials used in the interconnection and packaging of individual integrated circuits on a wafer. FEOL, on the other hand, involves the manufacturing processes used to create the actual transistor and other components on the wafer. Both segments play a crucial role in the overall semiconductor manufacturing process, with BEOL focusing on interconnection and packaging, and FEOL focusing on transistor and component creation.</p></p>
