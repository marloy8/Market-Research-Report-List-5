<p><p>Veterinary ICU cages are available in two main types: monoblock and parallel. Monoblock type cages are usually constructed with a single unit that includes an enclosed space for the animal, along with attached feeding and waste disposal systems. On the other hand, parallel type cages are designed with multiple units connected side by side, allowing for easy accessibility to each animal individually. Both types offer specialized features to meet the specific needs of veterinary ICUs, providing a safe and comfortable environment for recovering animals.</p></p>
