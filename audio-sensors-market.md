<p><p>Audio sensors are devices that convert sound waves into electrical signals. There are two main types of audio sensors in the market - analog output and digital output. Analog output audio sensors produce continuous voltage signals proportional to the sound waves they detect, providing a more precise measurement of sound levels. On the other hand, digital output audio sensors convert sound waves into digital signals, making them easier to process and analyze using digital systems. Both types of audio sensors have their own advantages and are used in various applications.</p></p>
