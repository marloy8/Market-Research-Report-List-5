<p><strong>The Fire Resistant Hydraulic Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFC Water-Glycol Fire-Resistant Hydraulic Oils</li><li>HFA High Water Content Fire-Resistant Hydraulic Oils</li><li>HFDU Synthetic Water Free Hydraulic Oils</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire-resistant hydraulic oils are classified into three main types based on their applications and compositions. HFC (Water-Glycol) oils are mainly used in industries where there is a risk of fire due to their high water content. HFA (High Water Content) oils are used in systems where a lower concentration of water is required. HFDU (Synthetic Water-Free) oils are suitable for high-temperature applications. Other types of fire-resistant hydraulic oils include HETG (Hydraulic Environmental Triglycerides) and HEES (Hydraulic Environmental Ester Synthetic) oils, which are biodegradable and environmentally friendly.</p></p>
