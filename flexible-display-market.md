<p><p>The flexible display market includes three main types: Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED), and Electronic Paper Display (EPD). LCD displays use liquid crystals to create images, while OLED displays use organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied. EPD, on the other hand, mimics traditional paper and does not require a backlight to produce images. Each type has its own strengths and limitations, catering to different market needs and preferences in terms of flexibility and display quality.</p></p>
