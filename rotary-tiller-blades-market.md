<p><p>Rotary tiller blades come in different types such as L Type, Zyrovator L Type, C Type, and J Type. L type blades are designed with a sharp cutting edge for efficient soil mixing. Zyrovator L Type blades have a curved shape for better soil penetration and mixing. C Type blades are designed with a slight curve to prevent soil buildup. J Type blades have a more aggressive design for deep soil disruption. Each type of blade is suited for different soil conditions and farming applications.</p></p>
