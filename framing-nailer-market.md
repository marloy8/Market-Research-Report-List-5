<p><p>The framing nailer market can be divided into three main types: Clipped Head Framing Nailer, Round Head Framing Nailer, and Coil Framing Nailer. Clipped head nailers have a clipped head design allowing for more nails in the magazine. Round head nailers are known for their versatility and ability to use nails with full round heads. Coil framing nailers use a coil of nails instead of a straight magazine, allowing for more nails to be held and reducing the need for frequent reloading.</p></p>
