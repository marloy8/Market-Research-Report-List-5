<p><p>Mini skimmers are small-scale oil and grease removal devices used in various industries such as food processing, automotive, and manufacturing. The market for mini skimmers can be categorized into three main types: belt type, disc type, and others. Belt type skimmers use a continuous belt to remove oil from water, while disc type skimmers utilize rotating discs to separate oil from water. The "others" market includes various types of mini skimmers such as tube type and drum type, each offering unique benefits and applications.</p></p>
