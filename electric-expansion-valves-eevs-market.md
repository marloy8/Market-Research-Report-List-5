<p><p>Electric Expansion Valves (EEVs) are commonly categorized into two types: Electric Type EEVs and Electromagnetic Type EEVs. Electric Type EEVs are controlled by an electric motor, allowing for precise adjustment of refrigerant flow based on system requirements. On the other hand, Electromagnetic Type EEVs utilize electromagnetic force to regulate refrigerant flow. Both types of EEVs are essential components in refrigeration and air conditioning systems, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency. These valves play a crucial role in maintaining temperature control and energy efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
