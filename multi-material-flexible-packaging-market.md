Polyethylene is known for its flexibility and durability, while Polypropylene offers high clarity and barrier properties. Polyamide provides excellent puncture resistance, EVOH offers superior gas barrier properties, and PET is known for its strength and heat resistance. Other materials like aluminum foil and paper are also used in multi-material flexible packaging for their specific properties.</p></p>
