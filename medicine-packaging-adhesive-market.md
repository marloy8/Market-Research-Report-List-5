<p><p>Medicine packaging adhesive market can be classified into three types: solvent adhesive, solvent-free adhesive, and waterborne adhesives. Solvent adhesives use volatile organic compounds for bonding, while solvent-free adhesives are free from such chemicals, making them more environmentally friendly. Waterborne adhesives use water as a carrier for bonding, making them safe for both humans and the environment. Each type has its own advantages and is used in medicine packaging based on specific requirements and regulations.</p></p>
