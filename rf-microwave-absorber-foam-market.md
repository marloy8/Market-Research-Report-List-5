<p><ul><li>Low Permeability</li><li>High Permeability</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low permeability RF microwave absorber foam is designed for applications where high frequency signals need to be absorbed efficiently without leaking out. This type of foam typically has a higher density and is ideal for blocking electromagnetic interference. On the other hand, high permeability RF microwave absorber foam is specifically formulated to allow some signals to pass through while absorbing others. This type of foam is used in applications where selective absorption is required for proper signal transmission.</p></p>
