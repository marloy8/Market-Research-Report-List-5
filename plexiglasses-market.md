<p><p>Plexiglass is produced using various methods, including casting, injection, and extrusion. Casting involves the pouring of liquid acrylic into molds to create solid sheets, while injection molding uses high pressure to force molten acrylic into molds. Extrusion involves pushing acrylic through a die to create long, continuous sheets. Other markets, such as machining and thermoforming, also utilize plexiglass for custom applications. Each market type offers unique benefits and caters to specific industries and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
