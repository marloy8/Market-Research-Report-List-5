<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (CFRTP) composites are classified into two main types based on the precursor material used in making the carbon fibers: Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based CFRTP and Pitch-based CFRTP. PAN-based CFRTP composites are known for their high strength, stiffness, and toughness properties, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. On the other hand, Pitch-based CFRTP composites are valued for their high thermal conductivity and resistance to high temperatures, making them ideal for applications in aerospace and automotive industries.</p></p>
