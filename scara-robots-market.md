<p><p>SCARA robots are classified into two main market types: Single Arm SCARA and Dual Arm SCARA. Single Arm SCARA robots consist of a single arm that can move in the X, Y, and Z axes, making them ideal for tasks like pick-and-place operations in assembly lines. Dual Arm SCARA robots, on the other hand, have two arms working together to perform more complex tasks requiring greater dexterity and precision, such as assembly of small electronic components. Both types of SCARA robots are widely used in industrial automation for their speed, accuracy, and efficiency.</p></p>
