<p><strong>The Molds and Tool Ejector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spring</li><li>Electromagnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molds and Tool Ejector market types include spring and electromagnet ejectors. Spring ejectors are commonly used in injection molding machines to push the molded part out of the mold cavity. Electromagnet ejectors use an electromagnetic force to eject the molded part. Both types provide efficient and reliable ejection of parts from molds, with spring ejectors being more durable and cost-effective, while electromagnet ejectors offer fast and precise ejection control. Manufacturers can choose the appropriate ejector type based on their specific production needs and requirements.</p></p>
