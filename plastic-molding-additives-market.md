<p><p>Plastic molding additives are substances added to the plastic material during the manufacturing process to enhance its properties. Plasticizers are used to increase flexibility, stabilizers to prevent degradation, flame retardants to improve fire resistance, blowing agents to create a foaming effect, and nucleating agents to promote crystallization. Other additives include colorants, antistatic agents, fillers, and lubricants. Each type of additive plays a specific role in improving the performance and characteristics of the final plastic product.</p></p>
