Supercritical Pulverised Coal Combustion involves burning coal at high temperatures and pressures to increase efficiency. Fluidised Bed Combustion involves suspending coal particles in a bed of heated air to reduce emissions. Gasification converts coal into a synthetic gas for cleaner combustion.
