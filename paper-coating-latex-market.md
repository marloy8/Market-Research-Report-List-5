<p><ul><li>Emulsion Type</li><li>Solution Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paper coating latex market can be classified into two types: emulsion type and solution type. Emulsion type latex is made by dispersing the latex particles in water to form a stable emulsion. It is commonly used in coating applications due to its ease of use and ability to provide a smooth finish. On the other hand, solution type latex is made by dissolving the latex in a solvent, making it suitable for applications where water cannot be used. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different needs in the paper coating industry.</p></p>
