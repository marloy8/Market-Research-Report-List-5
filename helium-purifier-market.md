<p><p>Helium purifiers are used to remove impurities from helium gas to ensure its purity for various applications. There are two main types of helium purifiers: Heated helium purifiers work by passing the helium gas through a heated catalyst bed to remove impurities through a chemical reaction. On the other hand, helium cryogenic purifiers use a cryogenic distillation process to separate the impurities from the helium gas based on their boiling points. Both types of purifiers are essential in industries that require high-purity helium gas.</p></p>
