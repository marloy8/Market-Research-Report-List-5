There are two main types of pumps in the market: Constant rate pumps deliver a continuous flow of medication at a fixed rate, while programmable pumps allow for adjustments in medication dosage and delivery schedules. Both types of pumps are commonly used in managing chronic pain and neurological conditions.</p></p>
