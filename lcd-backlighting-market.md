<p><strong>The LCD Backlighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thin Side View</li><li>Side View</li><li>Top View</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LCD backlighting market can be categorized into three types based on the position of the LED lights in relation to the display panel: Thin Side View, Side View, and Top View. Thin Side View backlighting involves placing LEDs on the side of the display, resulting in a slim design. Side View backlighting places LEDs on the edges of the display panel, providing uniform brightness. Top View backlighting places LEDs directly behind the display, offering high contrast and visibility. Each type caters to different requirements and preferences in the market.</p></p>
