<p><p>Bone densitometers are devices used to measure bone density and strength. There are two main types of bone densitometers on the market: axial bone densitometers and peripheral bone densitometers. Axial bone densitometers measure bone density in the spine and hip, providing a comprehensive assessment of overall bone health. Peripheral bone densitometers measure bone density in areas like the wrist, heel, or finger, offering a more localized view of bone strength. Each type of bone densitometer has its own advantages and applications in diagnosing and monitoring bone health.</p></p>
