<p><p>Cell-based humanized mouse models are essential tools in biomedical research for studying human diseases and testing potential therapeutics. There are various types of humanized mouse models available in the market, including CD34 humanized mouse models, PBMC humanized mouse models, and BLT humanized mouse models. CD34 humanized mouse models are created by engrafting human CD34+ hematopoietic stem cells, while PBMC models involve engrafting human peripheral blood mononuclear cells. BLT models are created by engrafting human fetal liver and thymus tissues along with CD34+ stem cells. These models provide a more accurate representation of human immune responses and disease pathology in preclinical studies.</p></p>
