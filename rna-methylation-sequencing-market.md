<p><p>RNA methylation sequencing market includes various types such as radioisotope incorporation, thin-layer chromatography, mass spectrometry, differential enzyme/chemical-RNA interactions, bisulphite RNA sequencing, antibody-based sequencing, and Big Data market. Radioisotope incorporation involves labeling RNA with radioactive isotopes, while thin-layer chromatography separates methylated RNA fragments. Mass spectrometry analyzes RNA methylation patterns. Differential enzyme/chemical-RNA interactions detect methylation sites. Bisulphite RNA sequencing converts cytosine to uracil for methylation analysis. Antibody-based sequencing uses antibodies to identify methylated RNA. Big Data market involves analyzing large datasets for RNA methylation patterns.</p></p>
