<p><p>The residential heat recovery ventilation system market includes various types such as Thermal Wheel Heat Exchange, Plate Heat Exchange, and Others. Thermal Wheel Heat Exchange utilizes a rotating heat exchanger to transfer heat between air streams. Plate Heat Exchange uses plates to separate the incoming and outgoing air streams while transferring heat. Other types may include heat pump systems, rotary heat exchangers, or run-around coil systems. Each type offers an efficient way to recover heat and improve indoor air quality in residential buildings.</p></p>
