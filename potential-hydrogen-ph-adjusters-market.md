<p><p>Potential hydrogen (pH) adjusters market includes various types such as stabilizers, neutralizing agents, and others. Stabilizers help maintain the desired pH level in a solution by preventing fluctuations. Neutralizing agents, on the other hand, are used to balance acidity and alkalinity in a solution. Other pH adjusters may include buffers, which help resist changes in pH, or chelating agents, which bind to metal ions that can affect pH levels. These different types of pH adjusters cater to various needs in industries such as agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment.</p></p>
