<p><p>Handheld pyrometers are used in various industries to measure temperature quickly and accurately. The two main types on the market are infrared and optical pyrometers. Infrared pyrometers use thermal radiation to measure temperature without contact, making them suitable for high temperatures and hard-to-reach areas. Both types offer portable and user-friendly solutions for measuring temperature in industrial settings.</p></p>
