<p><p>The non-venting level sensor market consists of two main types: the float type liquid level sensor and the hydrostatic liquid level sensor. </p><p>The float type liquid level sensor operates by detecting the position of a float within a chamber as the liquid level changes. On the other hand, the hydrostatic liquid level sensor measures pressure at different depths to determine the liquid level.</p><p>Both types of sensors are commonly used in industrial applications to accurately monitor liquid levels in tanks and vessels.</p></p>
