<p><p>High Transparency Ultra Clear Glass Market includes two major types- Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled Glass is manufactured by pouring molten glass onto a metal surface and rolling it into a sheet, while Float Glass is produced by floating molten glass on top of molten tin, resulting in a smooth surface with high transparency. Both types are known for their exceptional clarity and transparency, making them popular choices for applications requiring maximum light transmission and minimal distortion.</p></p>
