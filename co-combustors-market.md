<p><p>CO Combustors market includes three main types: Can, Cannular, and Annular. Can combustors consist of individual combustion cans that are connected in a straight line. Cannular combustors have a circular design with individual combustion cans arranged in a circular configuration. Annular combustors feature a single continuous combustion chamber that surrounds the turbine. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, emissions control, and maintenance requirements, catering to different needs and preferences within the industry.</p></p>
