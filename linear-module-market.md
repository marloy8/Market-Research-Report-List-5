<p><strong>The Linear Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Screw Linear Module</li><li>Synchronous Belt Linear Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Linear Module market consists of different types of modules, with two main categories being Ball Screw Linear Module and Synchronous Belt Linear Module. Ball Screw Linear Modules use a rotating screw to convert rotary motion into linear motion, providing high precision and efficiency. On the other hand, Synchronous Belt Linear Modules utilize a toothed belt and pulley system to achieve linear motion, offering a cost-effective solution with good speed and accuracy. Both types of modules are popular choices in various industrial applications for their unique features and benefits.</p></p>
