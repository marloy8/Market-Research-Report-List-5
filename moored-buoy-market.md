<p><strong>The Moored Buoy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point Mooring (SPM)</li><li>Multiple Buoy Mooring (MBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Moored Buoy Market includes Single Point Mooring (SPM) and Multiple Buoy Mooring (MBM). In SPM, a single buoy anchored to the seabed is used to moor vessels for loading and unloading operations. On the other hand, MBM utilizes multiple buoys connected by anchors and chains to provide mooring for larger vessels. Both types offer efficient and secure mooring solutions for a variety of maritime applications, such as oil and gas transfer, offshore loading, and ship-to-ship transfer operations.</p></p>
