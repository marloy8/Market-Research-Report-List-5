<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transfer switches are devices that allow for seamless switching between primary and secondary power sources during a power outage. There are two main types of transfer switches: Automatic and Manual. </p><p>Automatic transfer switches detect power loss and automatically switch to a backup power source, making them suitable for applications where uninterrupted power supply is crucial. Manual transfer switches, on the other hand, require manual intervention to switch between power sources, making them more suitable for applications where occasional power outages can be managed manually. Both types play a vital role in ensuring uninterrupted power supply in various settings.</p></p>
