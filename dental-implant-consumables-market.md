<p><p>Dental implant consumables market can be categorized into various types such as implants, abutments, fixtures, and others. Implants are the actual dental posts that are surgically placed into the jawbone to support replacement teeth. Abutments are connecting components that attach the implant to the replacement tooth. Fixtures are used to secure the implants in place. Other consumables include tools and accessories needed for the implant procedure. Each type plays a crucial role in the success of dental implant procedures.</p></p>
