<p><ul><li>Standard Multilayer (ML)</li><li>High Temperature Multilayer (HTM)</li><li>Low Hysteresis High Temperature Multilayer (LHHTM)</li><li>Spin Valve (SV)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GMR sensors are categorized into different types based on their design and performance capabilities. Standard Multilayer (ML) sensors are the most commonly used type, offering reliable performance in various applications. High Temperature Multilayer (HTM) sensors are designed to withstand extreme temperature conditions without compromising accuracy. Low Hysteresis High Temperature Multilayer (LHHTM) sensors provide high precision with minimal hysteresis at elevated temperatures. Spin Valve (SV) sensors offer improved sensitivity and signal-to-noise ratio. Other types of GMR sensors may include specialized designs for specific applications.</p></p>
