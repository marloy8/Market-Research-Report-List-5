<p><p>Textile anthraquinone dyes can be classified into two main types: soluble and insoluble. Soluble anthraquinone dyes are water-soluble and easily dissolve in the dyeing process, resulting in vibrant and long-lasting color on fabrics. On the other hand, insoluble anthraquinone dyes do not dissolve in water and require special methods for application, but they offer excellent color fastness and durability. Both types cater to different market needs, with soluble dyes being more commonly used in everyday textile applications and insoluble dyes favored for their resistance to fading and washing.</p></p>
