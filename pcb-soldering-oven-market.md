<p><strong>The PCB Soldering Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wave Soldering Oven</li><li>Reflow Soldering Oven</li><li>Selective Soldering Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PCB Soldering Ovens are classified into three main types based on the soldering process: </p><p>1. Wave Soldering Oven: Utilizes a wave of molten solder to solder through-hole components to the PCB.</p><p>  </p><p>2. Reflow Soldering Oven: Uses a controlled heating process to melt solder paste and create a reliable solder joint between surface mount components and the PCB.</p><p>  </p><p>3. Selective Soldering Oven: Allows for precise application of solder to specific areas of the PCB, making it ideal for intricate or complex assemblies. Each type caters to specific soldering needs in the PCB manufacturing process.</p></p>
