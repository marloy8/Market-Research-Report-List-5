<p><p>Concentric tube heat exchangers are classified into two main types: Double Tube and Three-Tube. Double Tube heat exchangers consist of two tubes, one inside the other, allowing for heat transfer between the two fluids flowing through the tubes. Three-Tube heat exchangers have an additional tube, providing a more efficient heat transfer process. Both types are widely used in various industries such as chemical processing, HVAC systems, and power generation for their ability to efficiently transfer heat between fluids.</p></p>
