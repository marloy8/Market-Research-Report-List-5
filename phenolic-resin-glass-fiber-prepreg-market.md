<p><p>Phenolic resin glass fiber prepreg market can be classified into two types based on the form of phenolic resin used: solid phenolic resin and liquid phenolic resin. Solid phenolic resin prepregs are pre-impregnated with solid phenolic resin which requires heat and pressure to melt and cure. Liquid phenolic resin prepregs are pre-impregnated with liquid phenolic resin which cures at room temperature. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
