<p><p>Diaphragm couplings in turbo machinery are flexible couplings used to connect rotating shafts and transmit torque while accommodating misalignment. Single diaphragm couplings have one diaphragm which allows for limited misalignment. Double diaphragm couplings have two diaphragms for increased misalignment accommodation. Multiple diaphragm couplings have three or more diaphragms for even greater misalignment tolerance. Each type of diaphragm coupling offers varying degrees of flexibility and durability, making them suitable for different types of turbo machinery applications.</p></p>
