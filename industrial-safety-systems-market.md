<p><p>Industrial Safety Systems are crucial for ensuring the protection of personnel, equipment, and the environment in industrial facilities. Emergency Shutdown Systems (ESD) are designed to quickly shut down processes in case of emergencies. Fire & Gas Monitoring Systems detect and alert operators of potential hazards. High Integrity Pressure Protection Systems (HIPPS) prevent overpressure in pipelines. Burner Management Systems (BMS ensure safe operation of combustion equipment. Turbomachinery Control (TMC) systems regulate the operation of turbines and compressors for optimal safety and efficiency.</p></p>
