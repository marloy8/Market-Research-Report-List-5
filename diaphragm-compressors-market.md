<p><strong>The Diaphragm Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Two Stage</li><li>Multi Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm compressors are categorized into three main types based on the number of stages they have: Single stage, Two stage, and Multi stage compressors. </p><p>Single stage compressors have one diaphragm chamber and are suitable for lower pressure applications. Two stage compressors have two diaphragm chambers and can achieve higher pressures than single stage compressors. Multi stage compressors have three or more diaphragm chambers and are capable of even higher pressures, making them ideal for demanding applications that require very high pressure levels.</p></p>
