<p><ul><li>Electrical or Electrocautery</li><li>Hydrothermal</li><li>Balloon Therapy</li><li>Radiofrequency Ablation</li><li>Cryoablation</li><li>Microwave Ablation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Endometrial ablation systems utilize different technologies such as Electrical or Electrocautery, Hydrothermal, Balloon Therapy, Radiofrequency Ablation, Cryoablation, and Microwave Ablation to treat abnormal uterine bleeding. Electrical or Electrocautery systems use electric current to remove the endometrial tissue, while Hydrothermal systems use heated saline to ablate the tissue. Balloon Therapy involves inflating a balloon inside the uterus to remove the tissue. Radiofrequency, Cryoablation, and Microwave systems use radio waves, extreme cold, and heat respectively to destroy the tissue.</p></p>
