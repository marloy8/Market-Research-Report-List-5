<p><p>Post and parcel sorting equipment market includes different types of sorting machines such as Single Split Tray Sorter, Cross Tray Sorter, Single Push Tray Sorter, and Others. Single Split Tray Sorters use trays that split into two sections to separate packages efficiently. Cross Tray Sorters use a crisscrossing conveyor system to route parcels to their respective destination. Single Push Tray Sorters push packages onto different chutes for sorting. Other types of sorting equipment in the market include tilt tray sorters, sliding shoe sorters, and pusher sorters.</p></p>
