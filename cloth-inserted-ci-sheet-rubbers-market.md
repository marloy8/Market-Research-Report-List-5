<p><p>Cloth-inserted (CI) sheet rubbers are available in two main market types - single layer cloth and multilayer cloth. Single layer cloth-inserted sheet rubbers consist of a single layer of fabric inserted between layers of rubber, providing moderate strength and flexibility. Multilayer cloth-inserted sheet rubbers, on the other hand, have multiple layers of fabric inserted between rubber layers, offering higher strength and durability. Both types of cloth-inserted sheet rubbers are widely used in various applications requiring abrasion resistance, impact protection, and sealing properties.</p></p>
