<p><p>Foam adhesive films are commonly categorized into three types: water-based foam adhesives, hot melt adhesives, and solvent-based foam adhesives. Water-based foam adhesives are environmentally friendly and provide strong bonding for a variety of applications. Hot melt adhesives offer instant bonding upon application and are commonly used in packaging and assembly processes. Solvent-based foam adhesives have a high level of flexibility and are suitable for bonding uneven or irregular surfaces. Each type of foam adhesive film offers unique advantages for different applications in various industries.</p></p>
