<p><p>Multi-layering inductors are commonly classified into two types based on their core material: Ceramic Core Multi-layering Inductor and Magnetic Core Multi-layering Inductor. Ceramic core multi-layering inductors are popular for their high Q factor and stability, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. On the other hand, magnetic core multi-layering inductors are preferred for their high inductance values and low core losses, making them ideal for power applications. Both types cater to different market requirements, offering varying levels of performance and efficiency.</p></p>
