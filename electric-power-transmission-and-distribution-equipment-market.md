<p><p>Electric power transmission and distribution equipment market can be categorized into two main types: overhead transmission and underground transmission. </p><p>In overhead transmission, power lines are installed on poles above ground level, which is more cost-effective and easier to maintain but susceptible to weather-related damages. </p><p>On the other hand, underground transmission involves burying power lines beneath the ground, offering better aesthetic appeal and reduced environmental impact but at a higher installation cost and more complexity for maintenance and repair. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring reliable and efficient power distribution.</p></p>
