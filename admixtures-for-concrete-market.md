<p><p>Admixtures for concrete are chemicals added to the mix to enhance the properties of the concrete. There are various types of admixtures available in the market. Concrete water reducers are used to lower the amount of water needed in the mix without affecting its workability. Concrete expanding agents are used to increase the volume of concrete without increasing the amount of cement. Concrete accelerators help speed up the curing process. Other types of admixtures include air-entraining agents, corrosion inhibitors, and plasticizers.</p></p>
