<p><ul><li>Cylindrical Bore Self-aligning Bearing</li><li>Tapered Bore Self-aligning Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Double Row Self Aligning Bearings come in two main types: Cylindrical Bore and Tapered Bore. Cylindrical Bore Self-aligning Bearings have a straight inner bore, which allows for easy mounting and dismounting. Tapered Bore Self-aligning Bearings have a tapered inner bore that requires the use of an adapter sleeve or withdrawal sleeve for mounting. Both types of bearings are designed to accommodate misalignment and can compensate for shaft deflections, making them ideal for applications where shaft misalignment is common.</p></p>
