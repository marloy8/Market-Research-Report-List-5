<p><p>GIS software is available in two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise GIS software is installed and run on a company's own servers and infrastructure, providing full control and security but requiring maintenance and updates. Cloud-based GIS software, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing for easier scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. Both types offer powerful mapping and analysis tools, but businesses must consider factors like data security, accessibility, and IT resources when choosing between them.</p></p>
