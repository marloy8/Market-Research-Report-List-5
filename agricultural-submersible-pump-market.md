<p><p>The agricultural submersible pump market can be classified into different types based on their design and functionality. The main types include oil-immersed pumps, water-immersion pumps, dry pumps, and other specialized pumps. </p><p>Oil-immersed pumps are filled with oil to provide lubrication and cooling for the pump motor. Water-immersion pumps are designed to be submerged in water to pump it effectively. Dry pumps, on the other hand, do not require any external liquid for operation. Other specialized pumps may include solar-powered or sewage pumps tailored for specific agricultural needs.</p></p>
