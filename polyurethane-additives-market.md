<p><p>Polyurethane additives market consists of three main types: catalysts, surfactants, and fillers. Catalysts are substances that significantly speed up the chemical reaction between polyols and isocyanates in the production of polyurethane. Surfactants are used to reduce surface tension and improve foaming properties of polyurethane foam. Fillers are added to polyurethane formulations to enhance properties like strength, stiffness, and fire resistance. Each of these additive types plays a crucial role in the overall performance and characteristics of polyurethane products.</p></p>
