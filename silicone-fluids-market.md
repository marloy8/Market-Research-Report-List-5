<p><p>Silicone fluids are categorized into two main types: Modified Silicone Fluids and Straight Silicone Fluids. Modified Silicone Fluids are chemically altered to enhance specific properties such as thermal stability, lubricity, or water repellency. On the other hand, Straight Silicone Fluids maintain their original chemical structure without any modifications. Both types of silicone fluids are widely used in various industries including cosmetics, automotive, and electronics for their unique properties and versatility in applications.</p></p>
