<p><p>Metal cutting machines (MCM) can be categorized into various types based on their cutting technology. These types include laser cutting machines, waterjet cutting machines, plasma cutting machines, flame cutting machines, and others. Laser cutting machines use a high-powered laser beam to cut through metal. Waterjet cutting machines utilize a high-pressure stream of water to cut through metal. Plasma cutting machines use a plasma torch to cut through metal. Flame cutting machines use a flame to melt and cut through metal. Others include various cutting technologies such as abrasive cutting and ultrasonic cutting.</p></p>
