<p><strong>The Forged Steel Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forged Steel Check Valves</li><li>Forged Steel Globe Valves</li><li>Forged Steel Gate Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Forged steel valves market consists of three main types - Forged Steel Check Valves, Forged Steel Globe Valves, and Forged Steel Gate Valves. Forged Steel Check Valves are designed to allow flow in one direction while preventing backflow. Forged Steel Globe Valves regulate flow in a pipeline by opening, closing, or partially obstructing the flow. Forged Steel Gate Valves control the flow of liquids or gases by raising or lowering a gate, providing a tight seal when closed.</p></p>
