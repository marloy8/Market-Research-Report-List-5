<p><p>Intravascular imaging systems are advanced medical devices used for examining the inside of blood vessels. The two main types are OCT endoscopic imaging systems, which use light waves to create high-resolution images of vessel walls, and hybrid intravascular imaging systems, which combine different imaging modalities for a comprehensive assessment of vascular structures. These systems play a crucial role in diagnosing and guiding treatments for conditions like coronary artery disease, offering improved accuracy and precision compared to traditional imaging techniques.</p></p>
