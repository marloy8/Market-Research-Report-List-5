<p><p>The gaseous air freshener market consists of two main types: ozone and negative ion air fresheners. Ozone air fresheners work by releasing ozone molecules into the air, which effectively neutralize odors and purify the air. On the other hand, negative ion air fresheners use negatively charged ions to attract and remove pollutants and odors from the air. Both types of air fresheners are effective in eliminating unwanted smells and improving indoor air quality, offering consumers a variety of options to choose from based on their preferences.</p></p>
