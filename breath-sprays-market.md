<p><p>Breath sprays come in two main market types: cosmetic breath sprays and therapeutic breath sprays. Cosmetic breath sprays are primarily used for freshening the breath and masking odors resulting from food, tobacco, and other sources. They are often flavored and may contain ingredients like mint or cinnamon. Therapeutic breath sprays, on the other hand, are formulated to address specific oral health issues such as dry mouth, gum disease, or bad breath caused by medical conditions. They may contain active ingredients like antiseptics or moisturizers to promote oral health.</p></p>
