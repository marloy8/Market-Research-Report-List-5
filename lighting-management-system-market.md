<p><p>A Lighting Management System (LMS) is a network-based system that provides centralized control and monitoring of lighting fixtures within a building or facility. LMS allows users to adjust and automate lighting levels, schedules, and energy usage, leading to improved energy efficiency, cost savings, and overall comfort and productivity.</p><p>The Lighting Management System Market is experiencing significant growth due to increasing awareness about energy conservation, stringent government regulations mandating energy efficiency in buildings, and the adoption of smart lighting technologies. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.3% during the forecast period.</p><p>The latest trends in the Lighting Management System Market include the integration of LMS with Internet of Things (IoT) technology, enabling remote access and control of lighting systems through mobile devices or cloud-based platforms. Additionally, there is a growing demand for wireless LMS solutions, which offer flexibility, scalability, and ease of installation compared to traditional wired systems. Moreover, the emergence of advanced lighting control strategies such as daylight harvesting and occupancy sensing is driving the adoption of LMS in commercial, residential, and industrial applications.</p></p>
