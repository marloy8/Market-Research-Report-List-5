<p><p>AC contactors are designed for controlling AC circuits, while DC contactors are designed for controlling DC circuits. AC contactors are more commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications due to their compatibility with AC power sources. DC contactors are typically used in applications where DC power sources are present, such as in automotive, aerospace, and renewable energy systems. Both types of contactors serve the same purpose of controlling the flow of electricity in a circuit, but their design and specifications cater to their respective power sources.</p></p>
