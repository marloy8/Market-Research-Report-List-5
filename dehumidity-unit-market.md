<p><strong>The Dehumidity Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cooling Dehumidifier</li><li>Rotary Dehumidifier</li><li>Pipeline Dehumidifier</li><li>Electroosmosis Dehumidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cooling dehumidifiers utilize refrigeration technology to cool moist air, causing the moisture to condense and collect as water. Rotary dehumidifiers use a rotating wheel coated with a desiccant material to absorb moisture from the air. Pipeline dehumidifiers are connected to the building's ventilation system to remove excess humidity. Electroosmosis dehumidifiers use an electrical charge to attract and remove moisture from the air. These different types of dehumidifiers cater to various needs and conditions for effective humidity control.</p></p>
