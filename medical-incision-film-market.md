There are three main types of medical incision films available in the market - PU Film, PE Film, and Iodophor Film. PU film is breathable and flexible, allowing for easy application and removal. PE film is cost-effective and provides good adhesion to the skin. Iodophor film contains iodine, which has antiseptic properties to prevent infections. Each type offers unique benefits for different medical applications.</p></p>
