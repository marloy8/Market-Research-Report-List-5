<p><ul><li>Electrical Double Layer Capacitors</li><li>Pseudocapacitor</li><li>Asymmetric Supercapacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphene Based Supercapacitors Market consists of three main types: Electrical Double Layer Capacitors, Pseudocapacitors, and Asymmetric Supercapacitors. Electrical Double Layer Capacitors store energy through the separation of charge at the interface between the electrode and electrolyte. Pseudocapacitors store energy through fast and reversible surface redox reactions. Asymmetric Supercapacitors combine the advantages of both types by using different electrodes for each side, allowing for higher energy and power densities. Each type offers unique benefits for various applications within the supercapacitor market.</p></p>
