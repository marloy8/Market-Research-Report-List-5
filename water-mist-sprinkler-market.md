<p><p>The Water Mist Sprinkler Market includes various types based on their operating systems: Wet Pipe System where water is constantly stored and ready to release upon activation; Dry Pipe System where water is held back until a sprinkler head is activated; Deluge System where all sprinkler heads are activated simultaneously; and Preaction System where water is held back by a preaction valve until a smoke or heat detector is triggered. Each system provides unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the environment.</p></p>
