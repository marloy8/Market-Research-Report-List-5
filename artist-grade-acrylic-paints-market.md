<p><p>Artist grade acrylic paints come in various market types including Fluid Acrylics and Heavy-Body Acrylics. Fluid Acrylics have a more liquid consistency, making them ideal for techniques like pouring and dripping. They are known for their smooth application and vibrant colors. On the other hand, Heavy-Body Acrylics have a thick consistency, allowing artists to create texture and impasto effects. These paints are popular for artists who prefer to work with a more traditional style of painting and layering techniques.</p></p>
