<p><p>The Disposable Laparoscope market consists of various types of instruments essential for laparoscopic surgeries. Bipolar Instruments are used for cutting and coagulation of tissues simultaneously. Monopolar Instruments are used for precise cutting and coagulation through a single electrode. Trocars are used to create access ports for instruments in the abdominal cavity. Graspers are used for holding and manipulating tissues during surgery. Dissectors are used for separating tissues and organs. Each type of instrument plays a crucial role in ensuring successful laparoscopic procedures.</p></p>
