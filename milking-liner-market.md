<p><p>Milking liners come in various sizes based on the mouth-piece diameter, with common sizes being 21mm, 22mm, and 23mm. Each size caters to specific needs and preferences of dairy farmers. The 21mm liners are typically used for cows with smaller teats, while the 22mm liners are considered a standard size for most cows. The 23mm liners are used for cows with larger teats or for those requiring higher milk flow rates. Choosing the right size liner is essential for efficient and comfortable milking processes.</p></p>
