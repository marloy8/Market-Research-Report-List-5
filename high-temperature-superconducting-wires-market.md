Second Generation HT Superconductors typically consist of coated conductors made of thin films of superconducting materials such as yttrium-barium-copper oxide. These advanced materials offer improved performance at even higher temperatures, making them more practical for a wide range of applications including power transmission, medical imaging, and scientific research.</p></p>
