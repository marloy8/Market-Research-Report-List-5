<p><p>RTV Silicone Sealant market is divided into two main types based on their composition: Single Component Silicone Sealant and Double Component Silicone Sealant. Single component sealants come ready to use and cure through exposure to moisture in the air. Double component sealants, on the other hand, require mixing of two separate components before application and cure through a chemical reaction between the components. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
