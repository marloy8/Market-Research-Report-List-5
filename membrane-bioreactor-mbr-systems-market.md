<p><strong>The Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hollow Fiber</li><li>Flat Sheet</li><li>Multi Tubular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) Systems come in different types, including Hollow Fiber, Flat Sheet, and Multi Tubular. Hollow Fiber MBR systems utilize a porous membrane in the shape of hollow fibers, allowing for efficient filtration. Flat Sheet MBR systems consist of a flat membrane sheet that separates the solids from the liquid. Multi Tubular MBR systems use multiple tubular membranes for filtration. Each type offers distinct advantages, such as higher filtration rates, reduced fouling, and improved effluent quality in wastewater treatment processes.</p></p>
