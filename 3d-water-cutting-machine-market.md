<p><p>3D Water Cutting Machine market consists of two major types: Sandless Cutting and Abrasive Cutting. Sandless Cutting utilizes high-pressure water jets to precisely cut materials without the need for abrasive substances. On the other hand, Abrasive Cutting involves the use of abrasive particles mixed with high-pressure water to cut through hard materials effectively. Both types of cutting methods are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing for their efficiency and accuracy in cutting various materials.</p></p>
