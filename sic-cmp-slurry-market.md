<p><p>SiC CMP slurry is available in two main types - Bulk Removal Slurry and Fine Polish Slurry. Bulk Removal Slurry is designed for high material removal rates during the initial stages of the CMP process, effectively leveling out any surface imperfections. On the other hand, Fine Polish Slurry is used to achieve the desired surface finish and smoothness of the SiC substrate. Both types of slurry play a crucial role in the polishing process, ensuring the optimal performance of SiC-based devices and components.</p></p>
