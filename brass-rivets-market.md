<p><p>Brass rivets market include various types such as solid rivets, structural steel rivets, split rivets, blind rivets, flush rivets, and others. Solid rivets are one piece of metal that is hammered or pressed into place for a strong, permanent fastening. Structural steel rivets are used for heavy-duty applications. Split rivets are two pieces connected by a central pin or rod. Blind rivets provide a fast and easy way to join materials without access to the back side. Flush rivets are designed to sit flat with the surface once installed. Other types of brass rivets include tubular, semi-tubular, drive, and shoulder rivets.</p></p>
