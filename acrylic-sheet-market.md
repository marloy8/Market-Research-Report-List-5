<p><ul><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic sheet market is divided into two main types: Cast Acrylic Sheet and Extruded Acrylic Sheet. Cast Acrylic Sheet is manufactured using a process where liquid plastic is poured into a mold and allowed to harden, resulting in a strong and durable sheet with excellent optical clarity. On the other hand, Extruded Acrylic Sheet is made by pushing melted plastic through rollers to create a sheet, making it more cost-effective but with slightly lower quality in terms of clarity and strength compared to cast acrylic.</p></p>
