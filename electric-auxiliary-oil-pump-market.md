<p><p>The Electric Auxiliary Oil Pump market consists of two main types: Integrated Pump and Separate Pump. Integrated Pump refers to a system where the oil pump is integrated into the main engine, typically used in vehicles with start-stop systems. Separate Pump, on the other hand, refers to a standalone pump that provides additional oil pressure to the engine when needed, commonly used in hybrid vehicles to improve fuel efficiency. Both types are essential for ensuring proper lubrication and cooling of the engine components.</p></p>
