<p><p>Mechanical Individual Quick Freezing (IQF) Equipment market is segmented into two types: Spiral IQF and Tunnel IQF. Spiral IQF equipment utilizes a continuous freezing process where products move through a spiraling conveyor belt. This allows for high efficiency and continuous production. On the other hand, Tunnel IQF equipment involves placing products on trays or belts and moving them through a tunnel with cold air circulating around them. This method is suitable for larger volumes of products and offers uniform freezing.</p></p>
