<p><p>Healthcare temperature controlled packaging solutions can be categorized into two main types - active and passive. Active packaging solutions make use of external power sources such as batteries or electricity to regulate temperature inside the packaging. On the other hand, passive packaging solutions rely on insulation and phase change materials to maintain the desired temperature without the need for external power sources. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, and the choice between them depends on factors such as cost, duration of transportation, and temperature requirements.</p></p>
