<p><p>Graphite heat exchangers are used in various industries for efficient heat transfer. The market types include Shell and Tubes, Blocks, and Plates. Shell and Tubes heat exchangers consist of a shell with tubes running through it for heat exchange. Blocks heat exchangers have a series of graphite blocks stacked together for heat transfer. Plates heat exchangers use a series of graphite plates with passages for fluid flow. Each type of heat exchanger offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications based on their design and effectiveness in heat transfer.</p></p>
