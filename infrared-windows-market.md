<p><p>The Infrared Windows market is divided into two types based on the material used: Crystal Material and Polymer Material. Crystal Material windows are made from high-quality materials such as sapphire, germanium, and zinc selenide, providing superior optical clarity and durability for industrial applications. On the other hand, Polymer Material windows are made from synthetic materials like calcium fluoride and barium fluoride, offering cost-effective solutions for less demanding applications. Both types serve the purpose of protecting infrared cameras and sensors while allowing them to capture infrared radiation.</p></p>
