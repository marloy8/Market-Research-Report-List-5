<p><p>Monofilament absorbable sutures are available in various types, including catgut sutures, collagen sutures, and others. Catgut sutures are made from natural collagen fibers sourced from the submucosa of sheep intestines. Collagen sutures are derived from purified bovine tendon collagen. Other types of monofilament absorbable sutures may be synthetic in nature, such as those made from polydioxanone or polyglycolic acid. Each type of suture offers unique properties and benefits for different surgical procedures.</p></p>
