<p><p>Surface Mount Thermistors are a type of temperature-sensitive resistor that are directly mounted onto circuit boards. There are three main types of surface mount thermistors - End-Banded Chip Thermistors, MELF Style Thermistors, and Top/Bottom Terminated Chip Thermistors. End-Banded Chip Thermistors have metalized terminations on opposite ends, MELF Style Thermistors have cylindrical bodies and metal contacts on the sides, and Top/Bottom Terminated Chip Thermistors have terminations on the top and bottom of the chip. Each type is designed for specific applications and mounting configurations.</p></p>
