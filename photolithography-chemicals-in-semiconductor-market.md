<p><p>Photolithography chemicals in the semiconductor market consist of various types such as silicon wafer, photoresist, HMDS, and photoresist ancillaries. Silicon wafer is the substrate on which semiconductor devices are fabricated. Photoresist is a light-sensitive material used for patterning on the wafer. HMDS is a primer used to enhance adhesion between the photoresist and wafer. Photoresist ancillaries include chemicals used for developing, stripping, and cleaning processes in photolithography. These chemicals play a crucial role in the production of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
