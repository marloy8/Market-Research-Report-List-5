<p><p>Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) is a non-invasive imaging technique used to generate high-resolution cross-sectional images of biological tissues. There are four main types of OCT devices in the market: Catheter-Based OCT Devices, Doppler OCT Devices, Handheld OCT Devices, and Tabletop OCT Devices. Catheter-Based OCT Devices are used for intravascular imaging, Doppler OCT Devices provide information on blood flow, Handheld OCT Devices offer portability and flexibility, while Tabletop OCT Devices are used in clinical and research settings for detailed imaging. Each type caters to different applications and user needs in the medical field.</p></p>
